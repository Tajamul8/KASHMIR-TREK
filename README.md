## 🥾 Popular Treks

### Aru Valley Trek
![Aru Valley](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1506905925346-21bda4d32df4?w=800&q=80)
**Difficulty:** Easy to Moderate  
**Duration:** 2-3 days  
**Altitude:** 8,500-10,000 ft  
Explore the lush green valleys and alpine meadows of Aru Valley.

### Lidder Valley Trek
![Lidder Valley](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1506905925346-21bda4d32df4?w=800&q=80)
**Difficulty:** Moderate  
**Duration:** 3-4 days  
**Altitude:** 7,000-11,000 ft  
Experience the pristine beauty of the Lidder River Valley.

### Kolahoi Glacier Trek
![Kolahoi Glacier](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1506905925346-21bda4d32df4?w=800&q=80)
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Duration:** 5-7 days  
**Altitude:** 13,500 ft  
Challenge yourself with the stunning Kolahoi Glacier trek.

## 🎒 What to Pack

- Trekking boots
- Weather-appropriate clothing
- Sleeping bag (for multi-day treks)
- Water bottles (2-3 liters)
- First aid kit
- Sun protection (sunscreen, hat)
- Headlamp/flashlight
- Map and compass

---

## 🌟 Best Trekking Season

- **Spring (April-May):** Pleasant weather, blooming flowers
- **Summer (June-August):** Warm days, clear skies
- **Autumn (September-October):** Crisp air, stunning colors

## 📋 Essential Information

### Getting There
- **Flight:** Srinagar has the main airport (SXR)
- **Train:** Nearest major station is at Samba (48 km away)
- **Road:** Well-connected via National highways

### Accommodation
Multiple options available in:
- Srinagar
- Gulmarg
- Pahalgam
- Sonamarg

### Local Guides
Professional guides available in all major trekking areas for safety and better experience.

---

## 🏥 Safety Tips

✅ Acclimatize properly before high-altitude treks  
✅ Carry adequate water and snacks  
✅ Use proper trekking footwear  
✅ Start early in the day  
✅ Inform someone about your trekking route  
✅ Check weather forecasts  
✅ Follow Leave No Trace principles
